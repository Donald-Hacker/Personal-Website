Service in the Community

As an active member of my community, I try to get involved in multiple ways. During the school year, I participate in the Aggie Rotaract Club which allows me to give back to the Bryan/College Station through clothing drives, Adopt-A-Street, and fundraising events for various causes. During the Summer, I volunteered at the Malcolm Purvis Library to help run the children activity events, and at the Montgomery Recycling Center to help sort and crush recyclables. In my Boy Scout Troop, I had the opportunity to give back to my community through my Eagle Scout Project, in which I created a fireplace, benches, and a ceremonial bridge for the public.
